    Who are we at Acadship

    We are Acadship ;developed as an one stop solution for all your scholarship requirements. Our motivation lies in the firm notion of education being accessible to all irrespective of financial capabilites. Despite several scholarships pertaining to educational institutes all over the world, but often the underpreviledged learners are unware about them. Our aim is to connect students with the available scholarships . Hence, we are dedicated to giving the best of information, with a prime focus on dependability, quality and realistic solution. We are working to turn our passion for education into a booming website for scholarship information. We hope you enjoy the experience as we enjoy offering them to you.
